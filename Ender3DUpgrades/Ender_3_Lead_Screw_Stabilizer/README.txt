# Summary

A stabilizer for the top of the Z axis lead screw.  Uses a 608-2RS bearing to hold the lead screw and two M4*10 screws with hammer head nuts to secure to the top extrusion.

Moving the carriage to the top will hold the lead screw in place to ensure the correct lateral placement.
