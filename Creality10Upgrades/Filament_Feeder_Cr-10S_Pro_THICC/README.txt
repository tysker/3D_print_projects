# Summary

Kudo's to Cobnut3D for the great feeder. I just added a fatter block on the bottom bracket to give it a bit more rigidity. There's still a bit of play so it moves when there's retraction. Works great in PLA. I found it seats tighter in PETG and will last much longer than PLA. Printed a large 10+ hour print with zero issues. 
I use it in conjunction with this spool guide https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3059604 - they work really well together I think.
